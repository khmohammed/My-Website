--- About Me ---

My name is Khadija Mohammed, and I am an incoming first year student at York University for Information Technology. I was born and raised in downtown Toronto, Ontario however my ethnic background consists of Indonesian and Kenyan roots. I currently work at the Toronto Public Library as a temporary Site Monitor for the Leading to Reading program. I enjoy spending time outside with my friends and exploring new places around the city and beyond.
